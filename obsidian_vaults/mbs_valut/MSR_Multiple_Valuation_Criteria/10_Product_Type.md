# Product Type

## Fixed-Rate
- **30-year fixed**: Deepest MSR market, longest expected life, highest multiples.
- **15-year fixed**: Shorter expected life (fast amortization), lower multiples, but often lower prepay from rate cuts (borrowers already committed to fast paydown).
- **20-year, 10-year**: Niche, thinner market.

## ARMs
- **Hybrid ARMs (5/6, 7/6, 10/6)**: Prepay near reset date (borrower refis to avoid adjustment).
- Shorter expected life → lower multiples than 30-yr fixed.

## Government (GNMA)
- FHA, VA, USDA loans.
- Higher servicing fee (44 bps typical) but higher advance requirements (T&I on delinquent), buyout obligations.
- Multiples typically **lower** than conventional despite higher fee — due to advance & operational burden.

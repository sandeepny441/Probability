# Loan Balance Size

## Prepay Effect
- **Small balance loans (< $150k)**: Slower to refinance — closing costs proportionally larger, less economic incentive.
- **Jumbo conforming / high balance**: Faster prepay, borrowers more sophisticated.

## Cost-to-Service Effect
- Cost to service a loan is **roughly fixed per loan** (~$70–$90/year performing).
- Small balance: fixed cost eats a bigger % of servicing fee → lower net cash flow → **lower multiple**.
- Large balance: fixed cost proportionally smaller → **higher multiple** margin.

## Sweet Spot
- Mid-balance loans ($200k–$450k) often optimal.
- Very small balance servicing sometimes considered "uneconomic" and trades at discount despite slow prepay.

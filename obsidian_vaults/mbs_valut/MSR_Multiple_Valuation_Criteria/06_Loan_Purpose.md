# Loan Purpose

Why the loan was originated affects prepay behavior.

## Categories
- **Purchase**: Typically slower prepay early on; borrower just moved in. Preferred for MSR.
- **Rate/Term Refinance**: Borrower already showed refi propensity → faster future prepay.
- **Cash-Out Refinance**: Fastest prepay historically; borrower is transaction-motivated.

## Multiple Impact
- Purchase-heavy pools command **premium multiples** (0.2–0.5x higher).
- Cash-out refi pools discounted.

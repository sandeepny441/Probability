# Cost to Service

Direct operational cost of servicing each loan. Directly reduces net cash flow → multiple.

## Cost Benchmarks (per loan per year)
- **Performing**: $70–$100
- **30-59 DPD**: $150–$250
- **60-89 DPD**: $300–$500
- **90+ DPD / loss mit**: $1,000–$2,500+
- **Foreclosure / REO**: $2,500–$7,500+

## Scale Effect
- Large servicers achieve lower per-loan cost through automation, scale.
- Small servicers subservice out to gain scale economics.

## Cost Components
- Personnel (customer service, collections, loss mit)
- Technology (servicing platform: BKI Empower, MSP, Sagent, etc.)
- Compliance / audit / QC
- Corporate overhead allocation
- Interest cost on advances
- Insurance (E&O, cyber)

## Multiple Impact
- Every $10 reduction in per-loan cost can add ~0.1x to multiple.
- Buyer's own cost structure determines the multiple **they** can pay — winning bidders often have cost advantage.

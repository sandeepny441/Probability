# Subservicing vs. In-House Servicing

Decision on how to service impacts economics of MSR ownership.

## In-House Servicing
- Full control over borrower experience.
- Higher fixed costs; needs scale.
- Enables recapture, cross-sell.
- Regulatory & operational risk borne directly.

## Subservicing
- **Subservicer** handles day-to-day servicing on behalf of MSR owner.
- Common subservicers: Cenlar, LoanCare, Dovenmuehle, ServiceMac.
- Fee: typically **$5–$10/loan/month** performing; higher for delinquent.
- MSR owner keeps servicing fee minus subservicing cost.

## Impact on Multiple Buyer's Bid
- Buyers without infrastructure use subservicers to enter MSR market.
- Subservicing cost is a **known input** → cleaner underwriting.
- Recapture harder if borrower relationship is with subservicer.

## Hybrid Models
- Own servicing platform + subservice overflow.
- Special servicing for delinquent (Mr. Cooper, Rushmore for private label).

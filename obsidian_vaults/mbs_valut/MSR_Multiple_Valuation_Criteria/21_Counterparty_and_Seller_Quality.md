# Counterparty and Seller Quality

Quality of the seller/originator affects loan quality and rep-and-warrant recourse.

## Origination Quality Assessment
- Seller's historical **defect rates** (per Fannie/Freddie/GNMA QC feedback).
- **Early Payment Default (EPD)** history — proxies for underwriting rigor.
- Delinquency vintage curves vs. peers.
- Loan-level compare-groups.

## Reps & Warranties
- Seller reps at MSR sale (in addition to origination reps to GSEs).
- **Indemnifications** for compliance breaches, TRID errors, etc.
- Life-of-loan reps: unbroken.
- Recourse strength depends on seller **financial capacity**.

## Financial Strength of Seller
- Buyer discounts MSR if seller may not survive to honor indemnifications.
- Insolvency risk = uncovered loss risk.

## Track Record with Buyer
- Repeat sellers with clean history: premium multiples.
- New / distressed sellers: haircuts.

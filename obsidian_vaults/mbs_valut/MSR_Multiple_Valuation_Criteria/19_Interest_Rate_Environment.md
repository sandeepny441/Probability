# Interest Rate Environment

Macro rate environment massively affects MSR multiples industry-wide.

## Rate Level
- **Rising / high rates**: WAC OOTM → slow prepay → **multiples expand** (5.5–6.5x seen in 2022-23).
- **Falling / low rates**: WAC ITM → fast refi → **multiples compress** (2.5–3.5x seen in 2020-21).

## Rate Volatility
- Higher implied vol → higher optionality embedded in prepay option → hurts MSR (short option).
- MOVE index often used as proxy.

## Yield Curve Shape
- Steep curve: More refi optionality (borrowers refi shorter fixed → 30-yr).
- Flat/inverted curve: Less arbitrage.

## Rate Cycle Timing
- Late-cycle high rates: peak MSR multiples but rate-cut risk looms.
- Post-cut environment: multiples reset lower.

## Hedging
- MSR often paired with **IO strips**, **TBA shorts**, or duration hedges.
- Hedged MSR value may differ from raw MSR value.

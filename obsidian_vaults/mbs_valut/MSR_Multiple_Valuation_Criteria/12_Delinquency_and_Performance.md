# Delinquency and Performance

## Current Performance Status
- **Current / performing**: Full multiple applies.
- **30 DPD**: Slight discount, roll-rate risk.
- **60 DPD**: Deeper discount.
- **90+ DPD**: Servicing becomes cost center — advances, loss mit, foreclosure work. MSR value can be negative.

## Roll Rates
- Historical D30→D60→D90 transitions used to model expected delinquencies.
- Post-COVID forbearance experience factored in.

## Loss Mitigation Cost
- Delinquent loans cost **5-10x** more to service than performing (~$500-$2,000+/yr).
- Reduces net cash flow → lower multiple.

## Prior Modifications
- Modified loans: Higher re-default risk, often trade at discount.

## Portfolio DQ Profile Impact
- Portfolios with < 1% 60+ DPD: Premium multiples.
- Elevated DQ portfolios: Bulk trades price at deep discount or require credit indemnification.

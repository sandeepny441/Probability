# Ancillary Income

Revenue beyond the base servicing fee.

## Sources
- **Late fees**: 4-5% of overdue P&I.
- **NSF / returned payment fees**.
- **Modification fees** (limited under CFPB).
- **Assumption / payoff / reconveyance fees**.
- **Optional insurance / product cross-sell** (limited but present).
- **Escrow float income** (see [[13_Escrow_Balances_and_Float]]).
- **Interest on custodial P&I accounts** (short-term float between collection and remittance).

## Recapture Revenue
- If servicer originates the refi when borrower prepays → captures new origination profit + new MSR. See [[15_Recapture_Rate]].

## Modeling
- Typically expressed as ancillary income per loan per year (e.g., $20-$50/loan).
- Higher for delinquent portfolios (more late fees) but that's offset by cost.

## Multiple Impact
- Strong ancillary income streams can add **0.2–0.5x** to multiple.
- Servicers with cross-sell platforms (insurance, HELOCs) capture more.

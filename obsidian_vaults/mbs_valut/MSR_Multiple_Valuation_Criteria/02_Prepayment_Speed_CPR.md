# Prepayment Speed (CPR)

**Conditional Prepayment Rate (CPR)** = annualized % of pool paying off. The **#1 driver** of MSR value.

## Why Critical
- Servicing fees stop when loan pays off.
- MSR life = duration of servicing cash flows.
- Faster CPR → shorter life → lower PV → **lower multiple**.

## Prepayment Components
- **Refinance-driven**: Rate-and-term + cash-out refis (rate-sensitive).
- **Turnover**: Home sales (housing-market driven).
- **Curtailments/Partial**: Extra principal payments.
- **Defaults**: Involuntary prepay (buyout from pool).

## Modeling Metrics
- **PSA**: Standard prepayment ramp benchmark.
- **CPB (Constant Prepayment Base)**: Long-run assumption.
- **Refi S-curve**: CPR as function of rate incentive.

## Multiple Sensitivity
- Every +1 CPR of long-run prepay speed can move multiple by ~0.1–0.2x.
- Duration of MSR often negative — value falls as rates fall.

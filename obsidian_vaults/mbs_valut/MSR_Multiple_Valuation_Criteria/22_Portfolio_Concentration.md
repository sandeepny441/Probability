# Portfolio Concentration

Diversification affects risk profile of the MSR pool.

## Concentration Dimensions
- **Geographic**: State/MSA concentration → HPI, disaster, judicial risk.
- **Investor**: All FNMA vs. mixed.
- **Product**: All 30-yr fixed vs. mixed.
- **Vintage**: Single-year vintage → single rate exposure vs. laddered.
- **Originator**: Single seller vs. multiple.
- **Borrower**: FICO/LTV bucket concentration.

## Effect on Multiple
- **Diversified pools**: Lower risk premium → higher multiple.
- **Concentrated pools**: Higher risk premium unless the concentration is favorable (e.g., all low-WAC, non-judicial state).

## Natural Disaster Risk
- FL, TX, LA hurricane; CA earthquake/fire.
- Disaster forbearance = advance obligation spike.
- Buyers apply catastrophe scenarios in valuation.

## Correlation Risk
- Concentrated exposure = correlated defaults in downturn.

# Property Type and Geography

## Property Type
- **SFR (Single-Family)**: Standard.
- **Condo/PUD**: HOA risk, slightly higher default in stress.
- **2-4 unit / manufactured**: Discounted.

## Geography (State & MSA)
Massively impacts MSR value.

### Judicial vs Non-Judicial Foreclosure States
- **Judicial states** (NY, NJ, FL, IL): Foreclosure timelines 2–4+ years → **much higher advance obligations** → lower multiple.
- **Non-judicial states** (CA, TX, GA, AZ): Faster resolution (6–12 months) → lower advance burden → higher multiple.

### High-Cost / High HPI Volatility
- CA, WA, MA, NY: Faster prepay speeds, high sensitivity to rates.
- Slower / more stable markets: More predictable cash flows.

### State-Specific Rules
- Some states (NY, NJ, MA, WA) have onerous servicing regulations, higher compliance cost.

## Multiple Impact
- Geographic concentration risk can compress multiple.
- Diversified pools trade at premium.

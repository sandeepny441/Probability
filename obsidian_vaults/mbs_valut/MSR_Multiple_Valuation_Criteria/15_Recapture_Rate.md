# Recapture Rate

**Recapture** = when the servicer refinances the borrower into a new loan when they prepay, keeping (or replacing) the MSR.

## Why It Matters
- Offsets prepay risk — the biggest MSR value drain.
- Servicer with a **strong origination platform** and marketing engine captures 30–60%+ of refinancing borrowers.
- Pure "MSR-only" buyers (funds, non-originators) can't recapture → assign lower multiples.

## Impact on Multiple
- High recapture servicer (bank / Rocket-type): can pay **+0.5–1.0x** higher multiple.
- No recapture capability: base multiple.

## Modeling
- Recapture assumed as % of voluntary prepayments.
- Discounted for uncertainty (execution risk, borrower retention actually achieved).

## Strategic Consideration
- Explains why originator-servicers (Rocket, PennyMac, UWM, banks) dominate bulk MSR bids at peaks.
- Also why regulators watch MSR concentration — recapture creates ongoing customer relationship.

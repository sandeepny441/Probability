# Investor Type / Agency

Who owns/guarantees the underlying loan drives servicing economics.

## Fannie Mae (FNMA)
- 25 bps minimum servicing fee.
- Advances: P&I on delinquent loans (scheduled/scheduled).
- Standard servicing cost profile.
- Deep MSR market, most liquid.

## Freddie Mac (FHLMC)
- 25 bps minimum servicing fee.
- Similar economics to FNMA.
- Cash Remittance Types: Gold PCs (S/S), ARC.

## Ginnie Mae (GNMA)
- 44 bps typical servicing fee (25 base + 19 g-fee-like).
- **Full pass-through advances**: Servicer advances P&I regardless of collection.
- Non-performing loan **buyout obligation** at 90+ DQ — servicer must fund at par, hold on balance sheet.
- Much higher liquidity/capital needs → lower multiples despite higher fee.

## Private / PLS / Portfolio
- Non-agency: Highly variable, contract-specific.
- Bank portfolio: Held by originator; not typically sold as MSR.

## Multiple Comparison
- FNMA/FHLMC: **4.5x–5.5x** typical mid-cycle.
- GNMA: **3.5x–4.5x** typical.

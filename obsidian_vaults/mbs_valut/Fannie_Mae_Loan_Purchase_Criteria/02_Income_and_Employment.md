# Income and Employment

Fannie Mae requires evidence that borrower income is **stable, predictable, and likely to continue** for at least 3 years.

## Documentation
- **W-2 Wage Earners**: Most recent paystubs (30 days), W-2s (2 years), verification of employment (VOE).
- **Self-Employed**: 2 years personal + business tax returns, YTD P&L, business stability evidence.
- **Other Income**: Rental (Schedule E), retirement, Social Security, alimony, bonuses, commissions — require history + continuance.

## Stability Rules
- 2-year employment history preferred; gaps must be explained.
- Job changes acceptable if within same field or with income increase.
- Declining self-employment income may be disqualifying.

## Special Income Types
- Bonus/overtime/commission: 2-year average required.
- Rental income: 75% of gross (25% vacancy factor).
- Foreign income: strict documentation.

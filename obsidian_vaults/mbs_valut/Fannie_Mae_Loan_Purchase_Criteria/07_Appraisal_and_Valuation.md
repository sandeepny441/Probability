# Appraisal and Valuation

Fannie Mae requires reliable property valuation to support LTV calculations.

## Standard Appraisal
- Licensed/certified appraiser using **Uniform Appraisal Dataset (UAD)** forms (1004, 1073, 1025).
- Delivered via **Uniform Collateral Data Portal (UCDP)**.
- Collateral Underwriter (**CU**) risk scoring: 1.0 (low risk) to 5.0 (high risk).

## Appraisal Alternatives
- **Value Acceptance (Appraisal Waiver)**: For eligible refinances/purchases with strong data.
- **Value Acceptance + Property Data**: Waiver with third-party property inspection.
- **Hybrid Appraisals**: Desktop appraisal with property data collection.

## Comparable Sales
- Minimum 3 comps, typically within 1 mile and 6 months.
- Adjustments must be supported by market data.

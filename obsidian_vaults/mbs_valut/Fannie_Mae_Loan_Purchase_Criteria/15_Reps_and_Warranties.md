# Representations and Warranties

When selling a loan, the lender **represents and warrants** the loan meets all Selling Guide requirements.

## Key Reps
- Loan complies with all applicable laws (TILA, RESPA, ECOA, HMDA, ATR/QM).
- Data delivered is accurate.
- Property valuation is accurate and supported.
- Borrower income/employment/assets accurately documented.
- No fraud or misrepresentation.
- Clear title, valid lien position.

## Rep & Warrant Framework
- **Life-of-loan reps**: Cannot be relieved (fraud, data integrity, compliance, clear title).
- **Sunsetting reps**: Relief after 36 months of on-time payments (or QC review).
- **Independent Dispute Resolution (IDR)**: Framework for disputing repurchase demands.

## Consequences of Breach
- **Repurchase demand**: Lender must buy back the loan at par.
- **Make-whole**: Lender indemnifies loss.
- **Loss of approval status** in severe/repeated cases.

# Fannie Mae Loan Purchase Criteria — Overview

Fannie Mae buys mortgage loans from approved lenders in the secondary market. To be eligible for purchase, a loan must meet criteria defined in the **Fannie Mae Selling Guide**. These criteria protect loan quality, ensure conformity for MBS pooling, and manage credit/prepayment risk.

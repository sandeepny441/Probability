# Underwriting — Desktop Underwriter (DU)

**Desktop Underwriter (DU)** is Fannie Mae's automated underwriting system (AUS).

## Purpose
- Evaluates loan risk holistically (credit, capacity, collateral).
- Provides underwriting recommendation:
  - **Approve/Eligible** — meets Fannie Mae guidelines
  - **Approve/Ineligible** — good risk but violates a policy overlay
  - **Refer with Caution** — must be manually underwritten or denied
  - **Out of Scope** — cannot evaluate

## Data Inputs
- Loan characteristics (amount, LTV, product)
- Borrower credit report
- Income, assets, liabilities
- Property information

## DU Findings Report
- Lists conditions to be met
- Documentation waivers (e.g., "1 paystub instead of 2")
- Appraisal waiver eligibility
- MI requirements

## Manual Underwriting
- Allowed when DU cannot evaluate, but stricter overlays (lower DTI cap, higher credit).

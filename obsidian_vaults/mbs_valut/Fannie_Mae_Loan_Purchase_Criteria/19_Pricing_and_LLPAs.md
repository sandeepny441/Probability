# Pricing and Loan-Level Price Adjustments (LLPAs)

Fannie Mae prices risk via **LLPAs** — upfront fees added based on loan characteristics.

## LLPA Risk Factors
- **Credit Score**: Lower FICO = higher LLPA.
- **LTV**: Higher LTV = higher LLPA.
- **Occupancy**: Investment > second home > primary.
- **Property Type**: 2-4 unit, condo, manufactured home = adjustments.
- **Loan Purpose**: Cash-out refi = higher LLPA.
- **Product**: ARMs, high-balance loans = adjustments.
- **Subordinate Financing**: Higher CLTV can trigger adds.

## LLPA Matrix
- Published in Selling Guide, updated periodically.
- Cumulative: multiple LLPAs can stack (with caps).
- Expressed as % of loan amount, paid upfront by lender (usually passed to borrower via rate).

## Guaranty Fee (G-Fee)
- Ongoing fee Fannie Mae collects to guarantee MBS.
- Component of MBS pricing; separate from LLPAs.

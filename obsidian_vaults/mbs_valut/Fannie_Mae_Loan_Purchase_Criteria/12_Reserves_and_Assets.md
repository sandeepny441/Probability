# Reserves and Assets

Reserves = liquid assets remaining after closing, measured in months of PITI.

## Reserve Requirements
- **Primary residence, 1-unit**: Often 0 months (DU-driven).
- **Primary, 2-4 unit**: 6 months.
- **Second home**: 2 months.
- **Investment property**: 6 months per financed property.
- **Multiple financed properties**: Additional reserves scale up.

## Eligible Assets
- Checking, savings, money market
- Stocks, bonds, mutual funds (70% of value typical)
- Retirement accounts (60–70% of vested balance if borrower is < retirement age)
- Cash value of life insurance
- Gift funds (with gift letter; source documentation)

## Ineligible / Restricted
- Cash on hand (unsourced)
- Unsecured borrowed funds
- Cryptocurrency (must be liquidated and sourced)

## Sourcing & Seasoning
- 60 days of statements typically required.
- Large deposits must be sourced and explained.

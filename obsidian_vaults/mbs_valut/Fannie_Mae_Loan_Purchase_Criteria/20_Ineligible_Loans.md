# Ineligible Loans

Loans Fannie Mae **will NOT** purchase.

## By Loan Characteristic
- Loans exceeding [[05_Loan_Limits_Conforming]] (jumbo)
- Interest-only, negative amortization, balloon (with exceptions)
- Loans with prepayment penalties
- Terms > 30 years (except modifications)
- Non-QM loans
- Sub-prime / high-cost (HOEPA) loans

## By Property
- Working farms, commercial properties
- Non-warrantable condos
- Manufactured homes without permanent foundation
- Properties with serious condition issues (C5/C6, Q6)
- Timeshares, houseboats

## By Borrower / Documentation
- No-doc / stated-income loans
- Loans with unresolved fraud indicators
- Borrowers on federal exclusion lists (SAM, OFAC)
- Foreign nationals without qualifying documentation
- Loans where borrower lacks legal capacity

## By Structure
- Loans not compliant with ATR/QM
- Loans with unacceptable subordinate financing terms
- Loans with private-party or non-arm's-length issues (some purchase transactions)
- Loans where seller/lender is not Fannie-approved

# Occupancy Types

Occupancy affects pricing, LTV limits, and reserve requirements.

## Categories
- **Primary Residence**: Borrower occupies within 60 days, majority of year. Best pricing, highest LTV (up to 97%).
- **Second Home**: Borrower occupies part of year. Must be 1-unit, suitable year-round, not a rental. Max 90% LTV.
- **Investment Property**: Rented out. Higher LLPAs, lower LTV (75–85%), more reserves required (6 months per property typical).

## Verification
- Occupancy declared on loan application; misrepresentation = fraud.
- Post-close occupancy checks may occur under [[16_Quality_Control]].

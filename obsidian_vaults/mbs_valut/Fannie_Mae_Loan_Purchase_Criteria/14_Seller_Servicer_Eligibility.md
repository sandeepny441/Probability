# Seller/Servicer Eligibility

Fannie Mae only buys loans from **approved seller/servicers**.

## Approval Requirements
- **Net Worth**: Minimum $2.5M + 0.25% of UPB serviced (adjusted periodically).
- **Liquidity**: 3.5 bps of Agency servicing UPB in liquid assets.
- **Capital Ratio**: Tangible net worth / total assets ≥ 6%.
- **Fidelity Bond & E&O Insurance**: Required coverage levels.
- **Quality Control Plan**: Written, comprehensive [[16_Quality_Control]] plan.
- **Operational Capability**: Systems, staff, processes.
- **Compliance History**: Regulatory good standing.

## Ongoing Obligations
- Annual financial statement submission (audited).
- Ongoing performance monitoring (STAR program).
- Compliance with Selling Guide + Servicing Guide.

## Types
- **Seller-only**: Sells but doesn't service.
- **Servicer-only**: Services but doesn't originate.
- **Seller/Servicer**: Both roles.

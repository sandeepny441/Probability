# Property Eligibility

The property securing the loan must meet Fannie Mae standards.

## Eligible Property Types
- 1-4 unit residential dwellings
- Condominiums (project must be Fannie-approved / warrantable)
- PUDs (Planned Unit Developments)
- Manufactured homes (with restrictions — must be real property, HUD tag, permanent foundation)
- Co-ops (limited markets)

## Ineligible Properties
- Working farms, ranches, commercial properties
- Log homes, earth homes (unless conforming to market)
- Timeshares, houseboats, motor homes
- Properties with less than 400 sq ft
- Non-warrantable condos

## Property Condition
- Must be "safe, sound, and structurally secure" (C1–C4 condition ratings).
- C5/C6 requires repairs prior to closing.
- Q1–Q6 quality ratings — Q6 typically ineligible.

# Conforming Loan Limits

Loan amount must be at or below annual limits set by FHFA for Fannie Mae to purchase.

## 2024 Baseline (1-unit)
- **Baseline conforming**: $766,550
- **2-unit**: $981,500
- **3-unit**: $1,186,350
- **4-unit**: $1,474,400

## High-Cost Areas
- Up to **150%** of baseline (e.g., $1,149,825 for 1-unit in high-cost counties like SF, NYC, DC).
- Alaska, Hawaii, Guam, US Virgin Islands: statutory higher limits.

## Beyond Limits
- Loans above these = **non-conforming / jumbo**, not eligible for Fannie Mae purchase.
- Limits are updated annually based on FHFA House Price Index.

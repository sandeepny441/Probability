# Loan Product Eligibility

Fannie Mae purchases specific loan products.

## Eligible Products
- **Fixed-Rate Mortgages**: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30-year terms.
- **Adjustable-Rate Mortgages (ARMs)**: 5/6, 7/6, 10/6 SOFR-indexed ARMs (post-LIBOR).
- **HomeReady**: Low-income borrowers, up to 97% LTV, reduced MI.
- **HomeStyle Renovation**: Purchase + rehab loans.
- **HomeStyle Energy**: Energy efficiency improvements.
- **RefiNow**: Streamlined refinance for low-income borrowers.
- **High LTV Refinance Option (HIRO)**: Underwater refis (limited availability).

## Ineligible Products
- Interest-only loans (post-2014 largely discontinued)
- Negative amortization loans
- Balloon mortgages (with exceptions)
- Prepayment penalty loans
- Terms > 30 years (except 40-year modifications)

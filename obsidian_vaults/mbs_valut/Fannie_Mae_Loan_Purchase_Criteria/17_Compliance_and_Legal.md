# Compliance and Legal Requirements

Loans must comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws.

## Federal Compliance
- **ATR/QM (Ability-to-Repay / Qualified Mortgage)**: Loan must be a QM under CFPB rules.
- **TRID (TILA-RESPA Integrated Disclosure)**: Loan Estimate + Closing Disclosure timing & content.
- **RESPA**: Escrow, servicing disclosures, kickback prohibitions.
- **TILA**: Truth-in-lending, APR disclosures, right of rescission.
- **ECOA (Reg B)**: Non-discrimination, adverse action notices.
- **HMDA**: Reporting of loan application data.
- **Fair Housing Act**: Non-discrimination in housing.
- **HOEPA / HPML**: High-cost & higher-priced loan rules.
- **SAFE Act**: Loan originator licensing (NMLS).
- **BSA / AML**: Anti-money laundering compliance.

## State Compliance
- State usury, licensing, high-cost loan laws vary.
- Some states/localities have anti-predatory lending statutes.

## Prohibited Practices
- Steering
- Unfair, deceptive, or abusive acts or practices (UDAAP)
- Prepayment penalties (in most cases)

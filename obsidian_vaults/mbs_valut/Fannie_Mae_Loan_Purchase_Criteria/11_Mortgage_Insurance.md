# Mortgage Insurance (MI)

Required when LTV > 80% on conventional loans.

## Coverage Requirements
- **97% LTV**: 35% MI coverage
- **95% LTV**: 30% coverage
- **90% LTV**: 25% coverage
- **85% LTV**: 12% coverage

## MI Types
- **Borrower-Paid Monthly (BPMI)**: Most common, cancelable at 80% LTV.
- **Single-Premium (Upfront)**: Paid at closing.
- **Lender-Paid MI (LPMI)**: Built into rate, not cancelable.
- **Split-Premium**: Mix of upfront + monthly.

## Approved MI Companies
- Only Fannie-approved MIs: MGIC, Radian, Essent, Arch, Enact, National MI.

## Cancellation
- **HPA (Homeowners Protection Act)**: Auto-terminates at 78% LTV of original value.
- Borrower can request at 80%.

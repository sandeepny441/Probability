# Quality Control (QC)

Fannie Mae mandates lender QC processes to ensure loan quality.

## Pre-Funding QC
- Sample of loans reviewed **before closing**.
- Verify data, credit, income, appraisal.
- Catch defects prior to funding.

## Post-Closing QC
- **Random Sample**: Minimum 10% of closed loans (or statistical sample).
- **Discretionary Sample**: High-risk loans (low FICO, high LTV, self-employed, etc.).
- Review completed within **90 days** of month-end.

## QC Review Scope
- Re-verify income, employment, assets, credit.
- Field review of appraisal (may include desk review or full re-appraisal).
- Occupancy verification (mail-outs, calls).
- Compliance verification.

## Reporting
- Defect rates tracked and reported to senior management.
- Trends analyzed; corrective actions documented.
- Fannie Mae also performs its own **post-purchase QC** and may issue findings.

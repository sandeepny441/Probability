# LO Behavioral Red Flags

Specific behaviors of an LO that indicate elevated EPO likelihood.

## Red Flag Behaviors
- **Repeated lock extensions / floats** near closing — suggests LO gaming rate timing.
- **Very short lock periods (15-day)** at above-market rate — LO betting on quick refi.
- **Loans priced consistently 25-50 bps above par** across LO's book.
- **Same borrower NAME appears in aggregator's tape multiple times per year** — LO refinanced them.
- **High share of "no-cost" refis** in LO's production.
- **LO is also a broker at another shop** (rare but happens).

## Communication / Lock Behavior
- Locks primarily on Fridays / month-end (targeting bonus tiers).
- High cancellation / redraw rate on locks.
- Frequent product switches close to close.

## Investor Detection
- Post-close data mining on NMLS ID × time × EPO outcome.
- Comparing LO's SATO distribution vs. peers at same lender.

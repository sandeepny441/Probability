# Servicer Retention Strategies (Defending Against EPO)

Once a servicer has boarded a loan, they can actively work to prevent EPO.

## Retention Program Elements
- **Rate-watch alerts**: Monitor mortgage rate movements; when borrower's loan becomes ITM, proactively call/email.
- **Portfolio recapture team**: Dedicated LOs who refi the servicer's own book.
- **Streamlined refi flow**: Fast, low-cost same-servicer refi to minimize borrower friction.
- **Non-solicitation contract enforcement**: Legal action against sellers who solicit serviced borrowers.

## Segmentation
- Tier borrowers by EPO risk score at boarding.
- High-risk borrowers get white-glove retention outreach.
- Low-risk borrowers get standard servicing only.

## Data-Driven Tactics
- Monitor **credit inquiries** on serviced borrowers (via credit bureau alerts) — mortgage inquiry = imminent refi → immediate retention call.
- Track competitor rate offers via monitoring services.
- **Payoff request** at title company = last-chance retention window (typically 5-10 days).

## Escrow Analysis / Life Events
- Detect borrower moves via mail returned undeliverable → outreach for HELOC on new home.
- Property tax data feeds for new-purchase indicators.

## Ancillary Products
- HELOC cross-sell keeps borrower in-house rather than cash-out refi elsewhere.

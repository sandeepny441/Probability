# Lender EPO History (Track Record)

**The single best lender-level EPO predictor is their own historical EPO rate.**

## What's Tracked
- **EPO rate by month/quarter**: % of purchased loans that pay off within 3/6/9 months.
- **EPO rate by product** (FHA/VA typically higher).
- **EPO rate by channel** (correspondent/broker).
- **EPO rate by LO** (NMLS ID granularity).
- **EPO rate trend over time**.

## Benchmarks
- **Industry average conforming EPO** (3-month window): typically 0.5-2%, spikes to 5-10%+ in falling rate environments.
- **FHA/VA EPO**: often 2-3x conforming rate due to streamline programs.
- **Well-managed retail lender**: 0.3-0.8%.
- **Problem lenders**: 5-15%+ EPO rates observed at some correspondents.

## Aggregator Consequences
- Poor EPO history → suspended purchase relationship, tighter clauses, worse pricing, indemnification demands.
- Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac also watch EPO — extreme cases trigger seller scrutiny.

## Public / Industry Data
- Recursion Cognitive, Optimal Blue, and other data providers publish lender-level prepay metrics visible to investors.
- SIFMA "TBA-adverse" lists — lenders whose loans are excluded from certain pools due to fast prepay.

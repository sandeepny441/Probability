# Prepayment Penalty Presence

## Conforming / Government
- Prepayment penalties are effectively **prohibited on QM / agency loans** post-Dodd-Frank.
- Not a factor in most agency EPO analysis.

## Non-QM / DSCR / Investor Loans
- Prepayment penalties (2-5 year step-down structures common) are permitted and priced in.
- Presence of PPP **dramatically reduces EPO risk** — borrower would owe 3-6 months of interest as penalty.
- Absence of PPP on non-QM = high EPO risk (borrower can refi into conforming as they season).

## Investor Impact
- Non-QM investors specifically want PPP protection.
- Loans without PPP trade at discount in the private label space.

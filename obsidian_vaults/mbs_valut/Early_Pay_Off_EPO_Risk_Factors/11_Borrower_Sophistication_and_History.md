# Borrower Sophistication and Prior Refi History

## Key Signal: Prior Refinance Count
- **First-time borrower / owner-occupant**: Lower EPO risk.
- **Borrower with 1+ prior refi in past 5 years**: Elevated EPO — established behavior pattern.
- **"Serial refinancer" (2-3+ refis in 5-10 yrs)**: Very high EPO risk — proven rate-chaser.

## Sophistication Proxies
- High FICO + high income + high loan balance combo.
- Multiple properties owned.
- Employed in finance / real estate industry.
- Online / call center-originated loan (self-directed shopper).

## Length of Time in Home
- Purchase loan → recent buyer → moving costs make near-term move-out unlikely → low EPO from turnover.
- Refi on old home → borrower has equity + established pattern.

## Data Sources for Sophistication Signals
- Credit report shows prior mortgage payoffs (indicating refi history).
- Property records (Corelogic, ATTOM) show ownership history.

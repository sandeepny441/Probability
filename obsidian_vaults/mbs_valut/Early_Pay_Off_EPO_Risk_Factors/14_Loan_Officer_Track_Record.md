# Loan Officer Track Record

Individual LO (NMLS ID-level) EPO history is a real signal used by sophisticated investors.

## LO-Level EPO Rate
- Investors and correspondent aggregators track **NMLS ID-level EPO rates** across their book.
- Some LOs consistently produce 3-5x the average EPO rate.
- Repeat offenders get **flagged, limited, or de-signed** by aggregators.

## Reasons High-EPO LOs Exist
- **Churn-and-earn**: LO refis their own book repeatedly (or hands off to a partner who does).
- **Bad pricing hygiene**: LO consistently locks borrowers above market to maximize commission.
- **Trigger-lead partnership**: LO may be tipping off outside brokers.
- **Broker network**: LO works with brokers who shop the same borrower to multiple lenders.

## Investor / Aggregator Actions
- **NMLS-level scorecards**.
- Post-close audit flags for EPO clusters.
- Lender required to indemnify or buyback loans from specific bad LOs.

## Servicer Actions
- Retention teams target loans from known high-EPO LOs for aggressive outreach.

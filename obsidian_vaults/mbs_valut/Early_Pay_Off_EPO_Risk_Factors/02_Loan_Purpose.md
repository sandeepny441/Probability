# Loan Purpose

Purpose is one of the top-3 EPO predictors.

## EPO Risk by Purpose
- **Rate/Term Refinance**: HIGHEST EPO risk. Borrower already refinanced once — proven "refi behavior." If rates drop even slightly further, they refi again.
- **Cash-Out Refinance**: HIGH EPO risk. Transactional borrower; may refi again if rates fall or they need more equity.
- **Purchase**: LOWEST EPO risk. Borrower just moved in; less likely to be in the refi mindset; also lender likely a purchase lender (relationship-based).

## Investor View
- Investors pay LOWER premiums for refi-heavy tapes.
- Purchase-only pools trade at meaningful premium vs. refi pools.

## Servicer View
- Refi-originated loans expected to run off faster → lower MSR multiple.
- Retention/recapture programs prioritized.

## Special Situations
- **Cash-out for debt consolidation**: Slightly stickier — borrower solved a specific problem.
- **Purchase in falling-rate environment**: Purchase can still EPO if borrower plans to refi immediately (some new-construction rate buydowns are structured this way).

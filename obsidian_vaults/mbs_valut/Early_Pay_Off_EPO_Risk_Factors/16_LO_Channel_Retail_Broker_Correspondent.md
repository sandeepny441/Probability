# Origination Channel: Retail vs. Broker vs. Correspondent

Channel is one of the strongest EPO predictors at the pool level.

## Channels & EPO Risk

### Retail (Direct Lender's Own LOs)
- **Baseline EPO**. Lender has retention team, ongoing relationship.
- Bank retail (chase, wells, BofA) usually lowest EPO — sticky depositor relationship.

### Broker / Wholesale (TPO)
- **HIGHEST EPO risk**. Broker's economic incentive is transaction, not relationship.
- Broker keeps borrower's contact info; refis them at next opportunity to earn again.
- Same broker may shop the borrower across multiple wholesale lenders → EPO risk elevated for whichever wholesaler ends up holding the loan.

### Correspondent
- **Elevated EPO risk**. Correspondent originates then sells; they have no incentive to prevent refi and often actively refi their own book to earn again.
- Correspondents with big refi teams (independent mortgage banks) are notorious sources of EPO to aggregators.

## Investor Screening
- Aggregators segment purchase decisions by channel.
- Broker-originated loans often carry **lower purchase premiums** or stricter EPO clauses.

## Wholesale Lender Practices
- Some wholesale lenders (UWM's "All-In Initiative") explicitly discourage brokers from refi'ing loans back through them.

# Borrower Occupancy and Property Use

## EPO Risk by Occupancy
- **Primary Residence**: Baseline behavior. Purchase = low EPO; refi = moderate.
- **Second Home**: Elevated EPO — financially sophisticated borrower with multiple properties.
- **Investment Property**: **HIGH EPO risk** — investors are transaction-motivated, cash-flow driven, refi frequently, cash-out to buy next property. Also DSCR loans → refi to conforming as property seasons.

## Occupancy Misrepresentation Risk
- Occupancy fraud (stating primary but actually investment) shows up as EPO: borrower refis quickly to correct classification or to release properties in a portfolio.

## Multi-Property Borrowers
- Borrower already owns 3+ properties → sophisticated investor → EPO risk elevated across all their loans.

## Investor Screening
- Investor / DSCR loan pools priced with higher assumed CPR / EPO than primary residence pools.

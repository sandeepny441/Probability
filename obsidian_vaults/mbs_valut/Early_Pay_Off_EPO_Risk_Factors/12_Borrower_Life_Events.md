# Borrower Life Events

Some EPOs are not refi-driven but **turnover-driven** (home sale).

## Life-Event Triggers for Fast Payoff
- **Job relocation shortly after purchase** — sale/payoff within months.
- **Divorce** — force-sale of home.
- **Death of borrower** — estate payoff.
- **Inheritance / windfall** — payoff of mortgage.
- **Rapid life change**: new baby → upgrade home; empty nesters → downsize.

## Signals in Loan File
- Application shows recent job change or short employment history at new address.
- Address history reveals frequent moves.
- Employment industry with high mobility (military, tech, oil & gas).

## Servicer View
- Retention/recapture strategies (which target refis) don't help against life-event EPOs.
- Portfolios with high military concentration (military bases) show elevated PCS-move turnover.

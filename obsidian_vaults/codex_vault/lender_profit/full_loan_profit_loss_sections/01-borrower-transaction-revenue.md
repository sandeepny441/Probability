# Borrower & Transaction Revenue

| # | Topic | Dollar amount | BPS | What it means |
|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| 1 | Origination charge | $900 | +23 | Fee paid to the lender for creating and arranging the loan. |
| 2 | Discount points | $800 | +20 | Upfront borrower payment used to obtain a lower note rate. |
| 3 | Application fee | $75 | +2 | Charge for accepting and initially reviewing the application. |
| 4 | Processing fee | $450 | +11 | Charge for collecting documents and moving the file through processing. |
| 5 | Underwriting fee | $650 | +16 | Charge for evaluating credit, income, assets, property, and loan eligibility. |
| 6 | Administrative fee | $300 | +8 | General lender charge for administrative work not separately itemized. |
| 7 | Verification fee | $100 | +3 | Charge for verifying employment, income, assets, or other application data. |
| 8 | Rate-lock fee | $125 | +3 | Fee for committing to a rate and price for a defined period. |
| 9 | Lock extension fee | $90 | +2 | Borrower-paid amount for extending an expiring rate lock. |
| 10 | Float-down fee | $65 | +2 | Fee for allowing the borrower to move to a lower rate after locking. |
| 11 | Document fee | $75 | +2 | Charge for preparing or handling closing documents. |
| 12 | Funding fee | $125 | +3 | Charge associated with preparing and releasing loan funds. |
| 13 | Interim interest | $220 | +6 | Interest collected from closing through the end of the closing month. |
| 14 | Ancillary closing fee | $60 | +2 | Other permitted lender revenue connected with closing the loan. |

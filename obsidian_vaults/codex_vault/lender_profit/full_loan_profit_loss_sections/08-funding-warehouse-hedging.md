# Funding, Warehouse & Hedging

| # | Topic | Dollar amount | BPS | What it means |
|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| 1 | Warehouse interest | ($300) | -8 | Interest paid while the loan is financed on a warehouse line before sale. |
| 2 | Line usage fee | ($60) | -2 | Fee based on the amount of warehouse credit actually used. |
| 3 | Commitment fee | ($20) | -1 | Cost paid to keep committed warehouse capacity available. |
| 4 | Warehouse wire | ($15) | -<1 | Bank fee for sending closing or payoff funds from the warehouse line. |
| 5 | Collateral-agent fee | ($10) | -<1 | Charge for controlling and tracking collateral pledged to the warehouse lender. |
| 6 | Wet-funding premium | ($25) | -1 | Additional cost for funding before the warehouse lender receives the original collateral. |
| 7 | Bailee fee | ($8) | -<1 | Fee for temporarily holding collateral for the warehouse lender during delivery. |
| 8 | Capital charge | ($80) | -2 | Internal return requirement for equity capital supporting the funded loan. |
| 9 | Liquidity buffer | ($30) | -1 | Allocated cost of maintaining cash and borrowing capacity for unexpected needs. |
| 10 | TBA bid-ask spread | ($55) | -1 | Trading cost between the price to buy and sell the hedge security. |
| 11 | Hedge carry | ($45) | -1 | Financing and time cost of maintaining a pipeline hedge. |
| 12 | Roll cost | ($35) | -1 | Cost of moving a TBA hedge from one settlement month to another. |
| 13 | Execution slippage | ($40) | -1 | Loss between modeled execution and the price actually achieved. |
| 14 | Pipeline fallout | ($65) | -2 | Hedge or capacity loss when a locked loan does not close. |
| 15 | Lock-extension risk | ($50) | -1 | Market-value cost created when the loan takes longer to close or deliver. |
| 16 | Pair-off fee | ($30) | -1 | Charge for reducing or canceling an outstanding secondary-market trade. |
| 17 | Option premium | ($25) | -1 | Cost of optional hedge protection against adverse rate movements or fallout. |
| 18 | Mark-to-market funding | ($20) | -1 | Cash and financing cost created by hedge margin or collateral requirements. |

# Pricing & Borrower Concessions

| # | Topic | Dollar amount | BPS | What it means |
|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| 1 | Lender credit | ($900) | -23 | Lender-funded amount used to reduce the borrower's closing costs. |
| 2 | Lender-paid points | ($300) | -8 | Discount points paid by the lender instead of the borrower. |
| 3 | Pricing exception | ($150) | -4 | Reduction from standard pricing approved to win or retain the loan. |
| 4 | Rate-match concession | ($125) | -3 | Cost of matching a competitor's lower rate or better price. |
| 5 | Lock extension absorbed | ($90) | -2 | Extension cost paid by the lender rather than charged to the borrower. |
| 6 | Float-down absorbed | ($80) | -2 | Cost of improving the borrower's locked rate without collecting the full fee. |
| 7 | Rate renegotiation | ($65) | -2 | Lost value from revising locked terms after market rates improve. |
| 8 | Appraisal promotion | ($550) | -14 | Appraisal cost paid by the lender as a promotion or concession. |
| 9 | Fee waiver | ($200) | -5 | Standard lender charge intentionally reduced or removed. |
| 10 | Closing-cost cure | ($75) | -2 | Lender payment used to correct a closing-cost issue. |
| 11 | Tolerance cure | ($45) | -1 | Amount paid when final regulated fees exceed permitted disclosure tolerances. |
| 12 | Disclosure cure | ($35) | -1 | Payment or credit used to remedy an inaccurate or late disclosure. |
